The Bhoramdev Vidyapeeth Coaching Program is a collaborative initiative of Vidya Bhawan Society, BALCO CSR and Delhi IAS Academy.

The program provides coaching for CGPSC and Vyapam examinations.

Library facilities are available.

Regular tests and mock tests are conducted.

Students absent for 10 consecutive days without notice may lose admission.

The program aims to support students from underserved communities.

Classes started on 19 May 2025.

The coaching centre is located in Kawardha, Chhattisgarh.
